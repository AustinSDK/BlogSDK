## About

Pripyat is located in the Kyiv Oblast providence of Ukraine, near the Russian border providing living space to the families of workers, working on the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant. Currently, fully being ran by the Soviet Union.

Pripyat contains multiple tourist attactions such as the Pripyat amusement park, along with the marvelous Polissya Hotel, or the Azure swimming pools!
